How to Take a Good Photo

For Condition Check

Capture a clear, side-profile view of the tyre. Ensure good lighting and that the entire tyre fills the frame for best results.

For Pressure Check

Take the photo from a low angle, showing where the tyre meets the ground. This helps the model see any flattening or bulging.
